Rules & Guidelines

— Ensuring a Safe & Fun Environment

Please review our rules before your visit

What You Need to Know

No Strollers

Strollers are not allowed in our café and play area. Please leave them in the designated area near the entrance.

Socks Required

Socks are required for everyone entering our café and playground area. Grip socks (from Kids Café) are required for all children playing.

No Outside Food

No outside food and drinks allowed. No eating, snacking, or drinking on the play area. Please use our café for all food and beverages.

No Birthday Celebrations

No birthday celebrations, gift exchanging, cake, cupcakes, or party items allowed during open play. For birthday parties, please book our private party packages.

Health Policy

We reserve the right to deny entry to anyone with fever or visible cold symptoms. If you are ill, please stay home and visit another time.

Rule Compliance

We reserve the right to deny entry to anyone not following our rules and regulations. No refunds will be provided for rule violations.

Important Notices

Signed Waivers Required

All participants must have a signed waiver on file. Parents/guardians must sign for children under 18. Waivers can be completed online or in person.

Supervision Required

Children must be supervised by an adult at all times. We are not responsible for unsupervised children. Please stay within the facility while your child is playing.

Time Limits

There are no time limits for regular play sessions. However, during peak hours, we may ask families to limit their stay to ensure all guests can enjoy our facility.

Photography Policy

Photography is allowed for personal use only. Please be respectful of other families and do not take photos of other children without permission.

Safety Guidelines

Safe Play

Our equipment is designed for children ages 0-12. Please ensure children use age-appropriate areas and equipment.

Gentle Play

Please encourage gentle play and respect for other children. Rough play, pushing, or aggressive behavior is not permitted.

Proper Footwear

Grip socks must be worn at all times in the play area. Regular socks are not sufficient for safety on our equipment.

Infant Areas

We have designated areas for infants and toddlers. Please keep older children out of these areas to ensure safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are grip socks required? ▼
Grip socks provide better traction and safety on our play equipment. They help prevent slips and falls while maintaining cleanliness in our facility.
Can I bring my own food for my child? ▼
No outside food is permitted. We have a full café menu with healthy options for children and adults. This policy helps maintain cleanliness and prevents allergies.
What if my child has allergies? ▼
Please inform our staff of any allergies. We offer various dietary options, but we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
Can I leave my child unattended? ▼
No, children must be supervised by an adult at all times. We are not a daycare facility and cannot be responsible for unsupervised children.
What happens if someone breaks the rules? ▼
We will first give a friendly reminder. If rules continue to be broken, we may ask the family to leave. No refunds are provided for rule violations.

Sign Your Waiver

All visitors must have a signed waiver on file. Complete your waiver online to save time at check-in!
