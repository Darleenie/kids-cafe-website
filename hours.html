Additional Information

Payment Methods

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. We also offer gift cards that can be used for admission and café purchases.

Reservations

Walk-ins are welcome! No reservations required for regular play. For birthday parties and events, please book in advance.

Age Restrictions

Our play area is designed for children ages 0-12. Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.

Socks Policy

Grip socks are required for all children in the play area. They can be purchased at our facility for $3 per pair.
